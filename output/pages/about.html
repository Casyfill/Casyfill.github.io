About

Hi! My name is Philipp Kats.

I am an urban data enthusiast with specific interest in data visualisation and decision making, as well as urban planning and architecture. Since 2016, I have been building data stories and tools around Real Estate and Urban Planning, mostly in the Python programming language.

I have a Specialist degree in Architecture (KGASU, Russia) and MS in Urban Informatics (NYU)

Through my career I worked as - an architect (2009-2012) - urban planner, lecturer (2012-2014) - data journalist, project manager (2012-2015) - data scientist, researcher (2015)

Projects

  • MosPlus (copy-cat of Cris Wong's PlutoPlus)
  • BranchPoint project; educational/research project in architectural computations (co-founder)
