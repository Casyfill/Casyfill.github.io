A talk at GeoNYC, New York's geospatial meetup, in 2018. The premise: official boundaries — zip codes, neighborhoods, council districts — rarely match how people actually search for a place to live. I showed StreetEasy's advanced search, which lets you draw your own boundary anywhere across the city and search listings inside it, with each custom boundary carrying its own name and owner. "Find your spot," literally: define the area that fits you, then look only there.
