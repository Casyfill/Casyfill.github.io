Hi! I'm Philipp — a Senior Applied Scientist at StreetEasy, where I work full-stack across several projects: our [home value estimate](https://streeteasy.com/home-value-estimate), matching, and recommendation — from the data and the models through to the services and APIs that ship them.

I'm drawn to tools and services somehow connected to the physical world. I got here the long way around — first an architect, then an urban planner, then a data journalist, and now — applied scientist. I've long stopped treating those as separate careers — it's one way of working that's just had several names.

Before moving to NYC, I co-created [Branchpoint](https://www.facebook.com/BranchPoint/), a non-profit teaching parametric design project, and perhaps one day I'll get back to it. And I teach at both university and school level.
