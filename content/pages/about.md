Title: About
Date: 2018-07-02 00:20

Hi! My name is Philipp Kats.

I am an urban data enthusiast with specific interest in data visualisation and decision making,
as well as urban planning and architecture. Since 2016, I have been building data stories and tools around Real Estate and Urban Planning, mostly in the Python programming language.

I have a Specialist degree in Architecture (KGASU, Russia) and MS in Urban Informatics (NYU)

Through my career I worked as 
	- an architect (2009-2012)
	- urban planner, lecturer (2012-2014)
	- data journalist, project manager (2012-2015)
	- data scientist, researcher (2015)
