Hi! I'm Philipp — I make tools and draw pictures.
The tools, lately, are at StreetEasy, where I work as Senior Applied Scientist on things like our [home value estimate](https://streeteasy.com/home-value-estimate), plus matching and recommendation. The pictures lean toward spatial analysis and visualization — a habit I picked
up the long way around: first as an architect, then an urban planner, then a
data journalist. I stopped thinking of these as separate careers long ago —
it's one way of working that's just had several names, and maybe there'll be
more to come.

I co-created [Branchpoint](https://www.facebook.com/BranchPoint/), a non-profit teaching parametric design project, and perhaps one day I'll get back to it. And I teach, at both university and school level.
