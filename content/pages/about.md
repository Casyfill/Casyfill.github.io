Hi! I'm Philipp — I make tools and create stories with data.

The tools, lately, are at StreetEasy, where I work on things like our [home value estimate](https://streeteasy.com/home-value-estimate), plus matching and recommendation. The pictures lean toward spatial analysis and data-driven narratives — a habit I picked up the long way around: first as an architect, then an urban planner, then a data journalist.

I co-created [Branchpoint](https://www.facebook.com/BranchPoint/), a non-profit teaching parametric design project, and perhaps one day I'll get back to it. And I teach, at both university and school level.
